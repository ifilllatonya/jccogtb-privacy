Delete Your Account

JCCOGTB Southern District App
Last updated: May 15, 2026

If you wish to delete your JCCOGTB Southern District account and have your associated personal data removed, follow the steps below. Your request will be processed within 30 days.

How to request account deletion

Send an email to:

contact@jccogtb.com

Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request

In your message, please include:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • Your username (if you remember it)
  • A short statement that you are requesting full deletion of your account

We may reply with a confirmation email asking you to verify the deletion request from the same email address as your account. This step protects your account from unauthorized deletion requests.

What data is deleted

When we process your deletion request, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your name, email address, username, and password hash
  • Your profile photo and banner image
  • Your bio and church affiliation
  • Your push notification token
  • Your authentication sessions
  • Your direct messages and private chat history
  • Your notification preferences and privacy settings

What data is kept

For the integrity of community conversations, the following content remains visible but is attributed to "Deleted User" rather than your name:

  • Posts you published to public community feeds
  • Messages you sent in public church chat rooms
  • Replies and reactions in public threads

If you want this content also removed, mention that explicitly in your email — we will review and remove it where possible (some content may be retained where required by law or to maintain the integrity of community moderation records).

Timeline

  • Within 7 days: we acknowledge your request by email.
  • Within 30 days: account and associated personal data are permanently deleted from our active systems.
  • Within 90 days: any remaining backup copies expire and are overwritten.

Questions?

Email contact@jccogtb.com — we're happy to clarify anything about this process before you submit a deletion request.
